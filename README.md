# National-Healthcare-Dashboard

🏥 National Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

Overview

The National Healthcare Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to provide comprehensive insights into patient trends, hospital performance, healthcare costs, and treatment outcomes across the United States.

This dashboard enables healthcare stakeholders, analysts, and decision-makers to explore data dynamically and make data-driven decisions for improving operational efficiency and patient care.

🎯 Key Objectives
Analyze patient admissions and demographics
Monitor hospital capacity and doctor availability
Track healthcare costs and billing trends
Evaluate treatment outcomes by medical condition
Identify operational and financial inefficiencies

📊 Dashboard Features
1. Key Metrics Overview
Total Admitted Patients
Number of Doctors
Rooms / Bedspace Availability
Average Billing Amount
Average Length of Stay (LOS)
Average Patient Age
Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons for performance tracking

2. Patient Demographics
Age group segmentation (18–24, 25–34, etc.)
Gender-based distribution (Male vs Female)
Blood group analysis (optional filter)
Helps in identifying target patient groups

3. Condition-Specific Insights

Filter by medical conditions (e.g., Asthma, Cancer, Diabetes)
View condition-specific:
Patient counts
Test results (Normal, Abnormal, Inconclusive)
Helps in understanding disease impact and trends

4. Hospital-Level Analysis
Compare performance across top hospitals like:
Cleveland Clinic
Mayo Clinic
Johns Hopkins Hospital
Metrics include:
Patient volume
Treatment distribution
Condition-wise breakdown

 5. Key Trends Analysis
Monthly patient admission trends
Admission types:
Emergency
Urgent
Elective
Day-wise patient distribution (Weekday vs Weekend)
Heatmap for monthly and daily admission patterns

6. Financial & Operational Insights
Revenue tracking by hospital
Insurance provider analysis
Average cost trends
Length of stay categorization:
Short Stay
Moderate Stay
Long Stay
Extended Stay

🎛️ Filters & Interactivity

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard by:

Year
Hospital
Medical Condition

This allows for customized analysis and deeper insights.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
Data Modeling – Relationships and calculated measures
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – For KPIs and YoY calculations
Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source
